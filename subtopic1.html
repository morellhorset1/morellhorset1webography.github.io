What is AI in Finance Matthew Finio and Amanda Downie AI in finance leverages technologies like machine learning to analyze data, automate tasks, and improve decision-making. It transforms processes like trading, fraud detection, risk management, and customer service. AI can perform high-speed, algorithmic trading based on real-time data, detect fraud by identifying unusual transaction patterns, and streamline loan processing by assessing creditworthiness efficiently. It also enhances customer interactions through AI-powered chatbots, personalizes financial planning, and provides advanced data analysis for market insights. Key players in AI finance include developers, CIOs, ethics officers, legal teams, and financial institutions, all working to ensure ethical and compliant AI use. Proper governance is essential for transparency, regulatory compliance, and responsible AI implementation, fostering trust and innovation across the industry.
